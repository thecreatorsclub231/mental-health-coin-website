Why $MHC

Mental Health Coin is a way for us to use modern technology in hopes to support people who suffer from mental illnesses, especially in a time where mental health is poorly funded. 100% of our initial revenue from MHC goes directly to our chosen charity and further donations will be placed along the way. Our inspiration has grown from Mental health awareness week (26 SEP – 3 OCT)as well as mental health day (10 OCT).
